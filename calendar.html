Calendar notes

How dates move when things switch between On and Off Track.

When work stays On Track

  • Dates are locked and appear with the green indicator.
  • Milestones link back to the On Track cards for context.
  • Recaps are added to keep the history clear.

When work shifts Off Track

  • Status switches to red and a reset date is assigned.
  • Owners document the blocker and the fix in Off Track.
  • Once resolved, the item returns here with the new date.
Calendar keeps the cadence visible. Jump to On Track for active delivery or Off Track for recovery work.
