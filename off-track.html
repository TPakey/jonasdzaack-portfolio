Reset plan

Steps to bring the Off Track items back into motion.

Diagnose

Identify the blocker, check scope, and confirm the owner. Timeline gets updated in the calendar.

Day 1

Design a fix

Create the next right action: new creative, a patched feature, or a customer loop.

Day 2

Ship and monitor

Push the fix, monitor metrics for 48 hours, then graduate back to On Track.

Day 3-7
Off Track exists to recover momentum. Check the Calendar for updated dates or head to On Track to see what is already moving.
